FAQS



How can I join LikHanap?

Just simply register as a freelancer or as a sole proprietor. Follow all the instructions and fill out all the necessary information. Here in Likhanap, artists would undergo a pre-screening test to make sure that the qualifications are met before we pass them to the clients. Once its done, wait for the approval/rejection email.


How to use LikHanap once I register as a freelancer?

Clients will post a commission project and the artists will apply for it and be evaluated based on their location, estimated price range, reviews, sample projects and our LikHanap pre-screening results.


How to use LikHanap once I register as a business owner?

Once Registered, the user will be able to choose from various freelancers who underwent pre-screening tests to make sure that they are qualified. The chosen freelancer would coordinate with the user so that they can come to an agreement for the commission. The user would have to pay a certain amount in order to post commissions that can be viewed by freelancers.


How does LikHanap bidding work?

As an artist, you will offer a price for your propose service to the client. While the other freelancers will also make a bid to the project and their bid maybe higher or lower than yours.


How can I find quality artists on LikHanap?

Simply make sure to look at the required skills and qualifications of the artists. Choose the best artist based on their profile details, reviews, portfolios, and past work experiences.


How will I communicate with my chosen freelancer to do my posted project?

It's up to you and the artist on how you will transact and communicate through the phone, skype, viber, email, or even messenger.


How can you ensure that the line of artists in LikHanap is real and a legit artist?

LikHanap has a pre-screening test to ensure that artists will surely meet the basic requirements for the project that the clients will offer.


How does the payment transaction work?

LikHanap offers two subscription package upon the usage of our website. The first package is a free trial that will last up to 3 project postings. The second one is our pro package that can last up to 100 project postings.


How can I make sure that a post of a business owner is legit and not a scam?

Here in LikHanap, we make sure that every business owners who registered is legit by looking into their business registration details, we will make sure that they are really posting a project by making them pay for every commission they post.
