FAQ



Reset Password


STEP 1: Proceed to Login page then click "Forgot Password".

STEP 2: Enter your registered email address.

STEP 3: A link would be sent to your email address which will redirect you to our Reset Password page

STEP 4: Enter your new desired password.

STEP 5: You can now log in with your new password.




Post an application accessible to the public


STEP 1: Click the "Post an application" button under the website preview on the Homestatus/Projects Tab.

STEP 2: Fill out the required fields on the application form. (Note: You can attach images of your previous commissions inside your application)

STEP 3: Click the "Submit" button.

STEP 4: After submitting the form, you can now see a preview of your application. You can click the "Update" or "Delete" button to change or dispose your current application, you can also click the "GO BACK TO HOMEPAGE" button to proceed to the homestatus.



Post an application exclusively to a specific business owner


STEP 1: Go to "Projects" tab.

STEP 2: Look from the list of project offers and click the "Apply" button to your chosen commission.

STEP 3: Fill out the required fields on the application form. (Note: You can attach images of your previous commissions inside your application)

STEP 4: Click the "Submit" button.

STEP 5: After submitting the form, you can now see a preview of your application. You can click the "Update" or "Delete" button to change or dispose your current application, you can also click the "GO BACK TO HOMEPAGE" button to proceed to the homestatus.




Inbox tab


STEP 1: Go to "Inbox" tab to see your confirmed applications.

STEP 2: Here you can look up the contact details shared to you by your commission partner.

STEP 3: Click the "Reply" button to give/make a response.




Verified Businesses

STEP 1: Go to "Projects" tab.

STEP 2: Choose 'Likhanap Verified' from the categories.

STEP 3: Likhanap will help you confirm the credibility of your commission partner and check if the business is really existing and officially registered.
